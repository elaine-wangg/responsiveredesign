Services

WellOne is the primary medical, dental and behavioral health provider of choice for the residents of the Rhode Island communities that we serve. As a result of our efforts, these residents have achieved the highest attainable level of physical, psychological and social well-being.

WellOne is Recognized as a National Leader in Clinical Quality Care Study Shows Care Provided by NPs and MDs in Community Health Centers Does Not Differ Our Principles and Values

We seek to be the leader in community-based primary care. We serve all patients regardless of race, color, national origin, religious affiliation, gender identity/expression, sex, sexual orientation, age, disability or ability to pay. We place an emphasis on health and wellness in addition to treatment. We emphasize public and community health. We are committed to reducing physical, social, cultural, linguistic and financial barriers to care. We have a firm conviction that a critical component of health care is providing patient education. We support patients’ individual choices and decisions that affect health care. We treat one another with respect. We maintain a work environment which promotes organizational excellence and which encourages employees to achieve their fullest potential. We support the standards set forth by the Bureau of Primary Health Care We recognize the interdependence of services and work cooperatively with others in the coordination of patient care. We work to transform the health care delivery and financing system through innovation and collaboration.
